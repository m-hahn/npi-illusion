Instructions

You will read a series of English sentences.

For this experiment, please place your left index finger on the 'e' key and your right index finger on the 'i' key.

You will read sentences word by word. After each sentence, we ask you to judge whether the sentence is an acceptable sentence of English. You will see two options: Yes (Acceptable), and No (Unacceptable). Press `e' (left hand) to indicate `Yes', and `i' (right hand) to indicate `No'.

You have 3 seconds for providing your answer. Make your choice quickly, but without making too many errors.

The system will alert you if you are making many errors. If you keep making too many errors, we might deny payment.

Example

In the task, you will judge whether sentences are acceptable English sentences. That is, judge whether these are sentences that someone might say.

The following are some examples of ACCEPTABLE sentences:

Kim has two gray dogs, and she takes them out every day.

Peter was greeted by the odd neighbor that he hadn't seen in a year.


The following are some examples of UNACCEPTABLE sentences:

The child are reading a book.

The scientist who Mary thought was intelligent.
